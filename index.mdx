Welcome to the Email-First Business knowledge base — your structured reference for building a sustainable solopreneur business with email at the centre, and for getting the most out of Kit (formerly ConvertKit) as your platform of choice.

This knowledge base is split into two complementary sections. The **Email-First Framework** covers the strategy, philosophy, and tactics you need to build and grow with email as your primary channel. The **Kit Guide** gives you practical, step-by-step instructions for every feature you'll use in Kit day to day.
